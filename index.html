Quickipedia

Quickipedia can help!

MyHive

A Honeybee Colony Tracking Tool

This ruby-on-rails web app was designed

for beekeepers looking for looking for an easier,

more efficient way to track their hives.

Using myHive allows beekeepers to record their

observations and visualize trends quickly and easily.

Technologies: Ruby on Rails, Materialize

Mesa

A Social Media Tool Connecting Chefs to Foodies

This web application, built in a three person team, connects chefs directly

to customers, allowing the personality and creativity of the chef to shine.

Foodies simply sign up with Mesa to follow their favorite chefs

and get rewarded with updates and deals from those chefs/eateries!
