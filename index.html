Sudoku

a two player sudoku game

Ever get drawn into a friend's sudoku puzzle and end up hunched over the same small phone screen?

Play with collaborative sudoku and the two of you can play on the same puzzle on your own screens.

The gameboard is live so you can instantly see eachother's moves and snowball to victory.

Gameboards are generated dynamically for endless fun!

Technologies: Sails, Mongo, Angular.js, Angular Material



Quickipedia

a wikipedia article summarizer

Quickipedia is the product of on one day hackathon. Having been introduced to Flask/React the day before,

my friend and I decided to practice what we learned in a fun and useful way.

Utilizing an Algorithmia algorithm we build an application that summarizes Wikipedia articles for you!

Want to learn something new? Ask for a random summary.

Technologies: Flask, React



MyHive

a honeybee colony tracking tool

MyHive is a data visulation tool designed for hobbyist beekeepers.

After each hive inspection beekeepers simply record their observations,

MyHive then uses this input to visualize trends quickly and easily - showing you what's really going on inside your hive.

Technologies: Ruby on Rails, PostgreSQL, Materialize



Mesa

a social media tool connecting chefs to foodies

Built in a three person team, Mesa is a social media tool that connects chefs directly to customers.

Foodies follow their favorite chefs and get rewarded with updates and deals from chefs annd their eateries!

Technologies: Node.js, Express, PostgreSQL, Bootstrap



The Chase

a heist game set on the london underground

This two-player front end game gives the detective two minutes to find the theif on the London underground.

If the theif eludes the detective during the two minute chase he gets away with his loot!

Technologies: HTML, CSS, Javascript/JQuery, Bootstrap
